About BHP Resources

A Legacy in Oil & Gas

BHP Resources Cooperation is a US-headquartered integrated oil & gas engineering, procurement & construction (EPC) company. From the deepwater fields of the Gulf of Mexico to major refinery complexes across three continents, we deliver technically demanding energy projects that set the industry standard for safety, efficiency and operational excellence.

With over two decades of experience, we specialize in the full hydrocarbon value chain — upstream exploration & production, midstream pipeline & transport infrastructure, and downstream refining & petrochemical processing. Our proprietary technologies and world-class workforce enable us to tackle the most complex challenges in the global energy sector.

Full Value Chain

Oil & Gas Services

End-to-end engineering, procurement & construction across the entire hydrocarbon lifecycle

Upstream

Exploration & production engineering for onshore and offshore assets. Deepwater platform design, FPSO topsides, drilling systems, well completions, and subsea production systems.

  • Deepwater & ultra-deepwater platforms
  • FPSO design & topsides engineering
  • Subsea production & SURF systems
  • Well engineering & completions
  • Enhanced oil recovery (EOR)

Midstream

Pipeline engineering, gas processing, storage terminals, and transportation infrastructure connecting production assets to downstream facilities.

  • Onshore & offshore pipeline systems
  • Gas gathering & processing plants
  • Crude oil & product terminals
  • Compression & pumping stations
  • NGL fractionation facilities

Downstream

Refinery design & optimization, petrochemical complexes, fuel processing, emissions control, and facility modernization for maximum yield.

  • Refinery EPC & modernization
  • Petrochemical & polymer plants
  • Hydrocracking & hydrotreating units
  • Sulfur recovery & emissions control
  • Tank farm & terminal operations

LNG & Gas Monetization

Liquefaction trains, regasification terminals, floating LNG, small-scale LNG, and gas-to-liquids technology.

Subsea Robotics & Inspection

ROV/AUV operations, diverless pipeline repair, SPRINGS® technology, AI-driven subsea inspection systems.

CCUS & Carbon Management

Carbon capture, utilization & storage. CarbonClear™ enzyme-based technology. 70+ CO₂ removal plants globally.

Asset Integrity & Maintenance

Life extension programs, turnaround management, integrity assessment, corrosion management, and digital twin monitoring.
